    04 / Interview

    Interview Preparation

    Common interview questions database by industry. STAR method response framework for behavioral questions. Mock interview practice. Body language and communication tips.

    Common Interview Questions

    Practice answers to the most frequently asked questions. Organized by job type: technical, behavioral, situational. Prepare your responses in advance to build confidence.

    Tell me about yourself.

    Keep it to 2 minutes. Structure: Past (education/experience), Present (current skills), Future (why this role)

    What are your strengths and weaknesses?

    Strengths: Pick 2-3 relevant to the job with examples. Weaknesses: Choose real weakness you are actively improving

    Why do you want this job?

    Research the company. Mention specific projects, values, or goals that align with yours. Show genuine interest

    Describe a time you faced a challenge.

    Use STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result. Focus on your specific role and the positive outcome

    The Muse Interview Guide Glassdoor Interview Questions LeetCode Technical Interviews

    STAR Method Framework

    Structure your behavioral interview answers with STAR: Situation, Task, Action, Result. This method keeps answers focused and shows your problem-solving process clearly.

    Mock Interview Practice

    Record yourself answering questions. Review your responses for clarity, confidence, and content. Practice reduces interview anxiety by 60% and improves performance.

    Click start to get your first question

    Speak your answer out loud. Time yourself: aim for 1-2 minutes per answer
